Dot-phrases

Type a dot-phrase in any note — for example .detox-followup — and it expands into the configured text. Personal phrases are yours alone; shared phrases reach every provider with one click.

Sharing a macro is a toggle, not a support ticket — the eCW gap this closes. Flip Scope on any row to move a phrase between private and clinic-wide.
