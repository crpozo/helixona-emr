How feedback works
1
Leave a note on any screen
Tap the floating Feedback button in the corner of any screen. Pick your name, write the note, and it saves instantly in your browser — no account, nothing to install.
2
Send it to Carlos
Send to Carlos opens an email that is already filled in — just press send. Or Export my notes downloads a file you can attach anywhere.
3
See it appear for everyone
Carlos merges the notes into the shared file (feedback/notes.json), and they show up for the whole team — as badges and a notes drawer on the exact screen you commented on. Comfortable with GitHub? You can propose changes to that file directly.
Every note shapes what gets built — an idea, a problem, a question, or an approval all count the same. The screens you see here change because of what you write.
